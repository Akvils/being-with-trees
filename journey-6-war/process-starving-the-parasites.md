#### Introduction

This is a process for reclaiming your personal energy from seven specific types of parasitic, non-physical entities. Their sole function is to feed on your core life force. They achieve this by projecting negative thoughts and emotions into your mind, provoking you into a reaction. #### The Mechanics of Control

These entities view Earth as a prize because humans possess an exceptional emotional range. They operate through energetic attachment, creating "implants" or "feeding tubes" in your energy field, often connected to a specific energy centre. This allows them to:

* Insert impulses that you mistake for your own thoughts.
* Amplify negative emotions to generate more potent energy.
* Harvest this emotional energy without your conscious awareness.

For millennia, most spiritual traditions have made a critical error: they treated these forces as internal psychological flaws (like the "Seven Deadly Sins") rather than as external parasitic beings. This misdiagnosis has allowed these entities to maintain their influence. By understanding them as external, you can finally revoke your consent and remove them.

**Your First Task: Identify Your Primary Attachment**

You are likely influenced by several of these entities, but one is always dominant. This is your primary attachment. As you read the descriptions that follow, pay close attention to your gut reaction.

* Which one triggers the strongest feeling of discomfort?
